About Us

Welcome to AgroAI, your one-stop destination for revolutionizing sustainable agriculture through the integration of fish farming, urban agriculture, and advanced AI technologies. We are a dedicated team of passionate individuals from Addis Ababa University (AAU) and Arba Minch University(AMU) in Ethiopia. Together, we've come together for the African Generative AI Hackathon hosted by A2SV, with a shared mission to transform the way food is produced and nourish communities while embracing cutting-edge solutions.

Our Vision

At AgroAI, we believe in a greener tomorrow where agriculture thrives in harmony with nature and technology. Our vision is to empower individuals, urban communities, and aspiring farmers to unlock the full potential of integrated fish farming and urban agriculture. By leveraging AI-driven insights, sustainable practices, and community engagement, we aim to foster a self-sufficient and eco-conscious approach to food production.
